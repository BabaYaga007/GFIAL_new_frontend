(Ground For Innovators And Learners)

GFIAL provides a platform where challenges related to technologies are proposed and are solved. Problems can be resolved by anyone who is willing to learn, practice and finally add to their existing set of skills.

GFIAL has three main assets, first is a student, second is the teacher and third is an Industry. The Platform mainly focuses on developing a bridge between educational institutions and organizations. It predominantly encourages innovation and aims at reducing the gap between academia and industries so that new ideas can be supported and industrial problem can be merged with academics. The problems at this platform can be solved by students and faculty members where the faculties members may also act as mentors. It pays attention to new ideas that are devised by the people and which can be inroduced to the market. Faculties can share projects with the students students and can seek also seek assistance if required. Industrial problems can also be posted on thr platform. It also intents to boost teacher-student interaction. GFIAL emphasizes on active participation of youngsters in the innovations and provokes them to not indulge only in theoretical knowledge but practical. It also supports real life problems solving which will let professional world get more skilled people in future and enhance the production sector.

We, at GFIAL intend to increase knowledge and skill set of students by proposing more projects that have much significance and a value in the market, having better understanding of concepts and promoting new ideas. GFIAL also subsidises the research and development if ideas are exceptional and worthy. Solutions to the proposed problem can be learnt as it will be solved or researched by users of our platform.
